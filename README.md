# 📊 Superstore Customer Analytics Project

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Superstore dataset** using Python to understand customer behavior, sales performance, product trends, geographic distribution, and customer segmentation using **RFM Analysis**.

### Key Objectives:
- Understand sales performance trends
- Identify top and low-performing products
- Analyze geographic sales distribution
- Segment customers using RFM model
- Generate actionable business insights

---

##  Dataset Information

**Dataset:** Superstore Dataset  

### Features include:
- Customer details  
- Orders and sales data  
- Profit information  
- Product categories  
- Geographic locations  
- Shipping details  
- Regional sales data

#  Week 2: Sales & Business Performance Analysis

---

## Subtask 1: Sales Trend Analysis

###  Objective
Analyze sales trends over time.

###  Findings
- Sales increased steadily from 2011 to 2014  
- 2014 recorded the highest sales  
- November showed peak sales  
- Seasonal fluctuations observed  

###  Business Insights
- Festive seasons increase sales  
- Promotions and discounts impact revenue  
- Certain months underperform  

###  Recommendations
- Launch seasonal campaigns  
- Improve low-season marketing  
- Focus on customer retention strategies  

---

##  Subtask 2: Product Analysis

###  Objective
Identify high and low-performing products.

###  Top Products
- Apple Smart Phone  
- Cisco Smart Phone  
- Motorola Smart Phone  
- Nokia Smart Phone  
- Canon imageCLASS Copier  

###  Business Insights
- Technology products generate highest revenue  
- High sales do not always mean high profit  
- Pricing and discounts affect profitability  

###  Recommendations
- Maintain stock for high-demand products  
- Reduce low-performing inventory  
- Improve pricing strategy  
- Focus on profitable products  

---

##  Subtask 3: Geographic Analysis

###  Objective
Analyze sales across regions.

###  Findings
- Top states contribute highest revenue  
- Urban cities dominate sales  
- Some regions underperform  

###  Business Insights
- Urban areas have higher purchasing power  
- Regional imbalance in sales exists  

###  Recommendations
- Expand operations in top regions  
- Improve marketing in low-performing regions  
- Use region-based strategies

#  Week 3: RFM Customer Segmentation

---

## Subtask 1: RFM Analysis

###  Objective
Analyze customer behavior using:

- Recency  
- Frequency  
- Monetary Value  

###  Process
- Selected reference date  
- Calculated RFM scores  
- Assigned customer scores (1–5 scale)  

###  Insights
- Recent customers are more likely to return  
- Frequent customers indicate loyalty  
- High monetary customers contribute maximum revenue  

---

##  Subtask 2: Customer Segmentation

###  Segments Created
- High Value Customers  
- Loyal Customers  
- At Risk Customers  
- New Customers  
- Lost Customers  
- Potential Loyalists  

###  Business Insights
- High-value customers drive most revenue  
- Loyal customers ensure steady growth  
- At-risk customers need re-engagement  
- Lost customers indicate churn risk  

###  Recommendations
- Loyalty reward programs  
- Personalized marketing campaigns  
- Discount offers for at-risk customers  
- Win-back strategies for lost customers
